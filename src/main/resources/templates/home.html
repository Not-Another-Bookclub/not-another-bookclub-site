Welcome to Not Another BookClub



"The best moments in reading are when you come across something...

a thought, a feeling, a way of looking at things – that you'd thought special, particular to you. And here it is, set down by someone else, a person you've never met, maybe even someone long dead. And it's as if a hand has come out, and taken yours.”

Alan Bennett,
The History Boys

Not Another Book Club (N.A.B.) seeks to connect people with each by making new friends or maintaining friendships through books.
It is, at its most basic level, a social-network for bibliophiles.

With N.A.B all the bookworms of the world can enjoy their pastime at any location.
There’s some days that traveling to the designated venue to enjoy another group book session is not possible.
